About Us

Driving Lebanon Forward with Innovation and Sustainability

ACTC Public Transport

ACTC Public Transport is Lebanon’s pioneering public-private partnership in urban mobility, launched to modernize and revitalize the nation’s transportation infrastructure. As a division of the Ahdab Commuting & Trading Company (ACTC), a trusted name in logistics and transport for over 55 years, we are committed to delivering safe, efficient, and eco-friendly transit solutions across the country.

Our Mission

To provide reliable, affordable, and sustainable public transportation that connects communities, reduces congestion, and enhances the quality of urban life in Lebanon.

What We Offer

Smart Bus Fleet: Our modern buses are equipped with advanced safety systems, including AI-powered driver monitoring, GPS tracking, and real-time route updates.

Digital Integration: We are developing a user-friendly mobile app in partnership with Katsh, enabling features such as live bus tracking, digital ticketing, and route planning.

Accessible Routes: Our initial lines, including B1, B2, B3, and ML3, serve key areas in Beirut and its suburbs, with plans to expand further.

Sustainability Focus: We are collaborating with local partners to construct eco-friendly bus stops using recycled materials, aiming to minimize our environmental footprint.

Our Vision

To establish a comprehensive and modern public transportation network that meets the evolving needs of Lebanon’s residents and visitors, fostering economic growth and social inclusion.
