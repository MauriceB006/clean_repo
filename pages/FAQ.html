Frequently Asked Questions

How can I track the real-time location of buses?

You can track the real-time location of buses using our official ACTC mobile app or website, which provides live GPS updates for all routes.

What should I do if I lose my bus card or ticket?

If you lose your bus card or ticket, please visit any ACTC customer service center or contact our support line to report and replace your card.

Are there any discounts for students or seniors?

Yes, ACTC offers discounted fares for students and senior citizens upon presenting a valid ID. Please check the fare policy page for more details.

Can I bring luggage or pets on the bus?

Passengers are allowed to bring small luggage and pets if they are in appropriate carriers. Large items require prior approval from ACTC management.
